## Privacy Policy

Phone Case Store respects your privacy. This page explains the basic information used to support browsing, ordering, payment, and customer service.

### Information We Use

When you shop with us, Shopify may process order details such as your name, email address, shipping address, billing information, selected products, and checkout activity.

### How Information Is Used

Information is used to process orders, provide checkout, manage cart activity, improve the shopping experience, and respond to customer support messages.

### Payments

Checkout and payment are handled through Shopify. We do not store full payment card details in this website codebase.

### Cookies

The site may use cookies or local browser storage for cart behavior, preferences, and basic storefront functionality.

### Contact

If you have questions about privacy or an order, contact us through the contact page.
